What is the CMTA?​

The CMTA (Customs Modernization and Tariff Act) exam is a mandatory licensure exam for aspiring customs brokers in the Philippines. Administered under RA 10863, it tests knowledge of:

  • Customs procedures
  • Import and export regulations
  • Tariff classifications
  • Duties and taxes

Passing this exam allows you to legally act as a customs broker and help importers navigate complex customs processes.

Common Challenges for CMTA Exam Takers​

Many aspiring brokers struggle due to:

  1. Disorganized Study Materials – Regulations and procedures are scattered across multiple sources.
  2. Complex Tariff Codes – Learning the ASEAN Harmonized Tariff Nomenclature (AHTN) and other classifications can be overwhelming.
  3. Time Management – The exam requires speed and accuracy under pressure.

Without a structured approach, even the most diligent students can miss key concepts.
