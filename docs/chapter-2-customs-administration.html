Chapter 2 – Customs Administration

This chapter outlines the structure and responsibilities of the Bureau of Customs under the CMTA.

🏢 Section 201 – Bureau of Customs​

The Bureau of Customs, under the Department of Finance, is the principal agency for implementing customs laws and collecting duties, taxes, and fees related to importation and exportation.

👥 Section 202 – Powers and Functions of the Bureau​

The Bureau of Customs shall:

  • Assess and collect customs revenues
  • Prevent and suppress smuggling
  • Supervise import and export cargo
  • Enforce customs and tariff laws
  • Facilitate trade and customs procedures
  • Promote transparency and professionalism

📌 Notes:​

These powers enable the Bureau to fulfill both revenue collection and regulatory functions in line with international standards.
